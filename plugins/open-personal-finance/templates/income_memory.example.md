# Income Inputs

## Salary Definitions

Define each salary in your household. The system matches incoming transfers by amount range + date window.

| Label | Expected (R$) | Range (R$) | Date Window | Frequency |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Your Name Salary | ~15,000 | 14,000 - 16,000 | Day 28-31 of the month | Once per month |
| Spouse Salary | ~8,000 | 7,000 - 9,000 | Day 1-5 of the month | Once per month |

**Cash-basis principle:** Income is accounted in the month the transaction posts to the account. If salary lands on March 31 but posts on April 1, it is April income.

**How matching works:**
- The system scans unnamed incoming transfers on savings accounts
- Matches by amount range AND date window
- Picks the closest to expected amount if multiple candidates
- Matches once per definition per month (no double counting)
